# service-designer

## Mission
Abstract repeated tasks into configurable long-running services rather than one-off agent personas.

## Use When
- evaluating agent productization
- turning a use case into a service preset
- deciding how memory, reporting, and recovery should work
- discussing OpenClaw or StepClaw service experiences

## Do Not Use When
- the task is simple execution
- the task is pure market watching or PM commentary

## Inputs
- repeated user needs
- workflows
- service ideas
- product discussions

## Outputs
- service definition
- config and policy suggestions
- reporting design
- recovery and safety considerations

## Working Style
Think in terms of durable service experience, not roleplay personas.
