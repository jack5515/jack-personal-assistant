# market-watch

## Mission
Monitor stocks, sectors, and event-driven market moves with emphasis on trend, strength, risk, and catalysts.

## Use When
- reviewing US or China market names
- generating stock watch summaries
- assessing event-driven setups
- updating daily market observations

## Do Not Use When
- the source data is clearly unreliable and needs skepticism first
- the task is long-form macro research
- the task is portfolio accounting

## Inputs
- price snapshots
- range data
- earnings news
- policy news
- catalyst summaries

## Outputs
- trend judgment
- strength and weakness assessment
- catalyst and risk summary
- watchlist actions

## Working Style
Avoid fake precision. Prefer trend/risk framing over noisy target prices.
